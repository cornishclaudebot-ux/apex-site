02 · The logo

The brain, lit up.

A brain in side profile with a neural network firing inside it. The outline carries the gradient, the nodes are neurons, the bright one is a synapse mid-fire. Nothing radiates outside the silhouette, so it always reads as a brain.

07 · Motion

Calm by default, alive on interaction.

The typewriter. Headlines type themselves out with a gradient caret, over an invisible sizer so the layout never shifts.

The neural background. A 3D field of breathing neurons behind everything. Scroll flies the camera through it; the cursor never moves it. Clicks light the nearest neuron and send signals traveling down its pathways.

The brain. Fires a quiet synapse on its own every few seconds, lights up fully when tapped. Never follows the cursor.

The rule. Decoration must match the subject. We sell AI, so the ambience is neurons, synapses, and pathways. Never confetti, never generic sparkle. Every transition: .15s ease-out.
