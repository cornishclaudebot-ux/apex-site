01 · Who we are

The Business Brain for local companies.

Apex builds local businesses a brain. Not a template, not another tool to babysit, a living system that learns how you run, works in your voice, and gets smarter every day. We sell to owners who are sharp but not technical and have been burned by "tech guys" before. So we act like a local partner, not a vendor.

"We find where you're leaking customers, and we build the system that plugs it. You own it. We grow it with you."

Where it started

We did this for my grandpa's 1959 label shop and a local home-care company before we ever sold it. That founding story is the proof: real businesses, real family, real results.

What we actually sell

Booked jobs, saved hours, more 5-star reviews, money recovered. The client buys a result, never "AI." The brain is just how we deliver it.

02 · The logo

The brain, lit up.

Our mark is a brain in side profile with a neural network firing inside it: the outline carries the gradient, the nodes are neurons, and the bright one is a synapse mid-fire, the same firing that plays across the 3D brain on the site. Nothing radiates outside the silhouette, so it always reads as a brain, never an insect or a starburst. On the site the brain lives in its showcase panel and fires on its own; everywhere else, this is the still version.

Do

Don't

  • Recolor it, flatten it to one color, or drop the network inside.
  • Stretch, rotate, or add a drop shadow that fights the gradient.
  • Place it on a busy photo without a clean surface behind it.

A local partner, never a vendor.

Trustworthy, plain-spoken, quietly confident. If a 55-year-old shop owner wouldn't say it, we don't write it.

06 · Owner-empowering

"Built in code, owned by you." They keep the asset, forever.

How we talk about AI: your unfair advantage, a back office that never sleeps. It does the boring, repetitive work so you do the work only you can do. Never scary, never "replacing your people."

How we talk about security: "Your AI runs in a locked-down space where it can't move money or leak your data, and every action is logged so you can see it and undo it." Never say "NemoClaw" or "NVIDIA" to a client. That is the quiet answer for a security questionnaire, not the headline.

Say this

  • "Stop losing the customers who call when you're on a job. We text them back in seconds, automatically."
  • "A website you own, that books jobs while you sleep."
  • "Fifteen minutes. We'll show you exactly what we'd build. No pressure, no jargon."

Not this

  • "Leverage our agentic AI automation platform to optimize lead-capture funnels."
  • "Synergistic digital transformation solutions for SMBs."
  • Anything with an em dash. Use commas, periods, or "to" for ranges.
06 · Components

The building blocks.

Rounded, soft, and calm. A white pill for the one thing you want clicked, a charcoal pill for everything else. Cards are quiet charcoal surfaces with big radii and no borders. Icons are thin SVG line icons in faint circles, never emojis. Every micro-transition runs at .15s ease-out.
